# My Daily Steps Log

Transform your daily walking into meaningful progress with My Daily Steps Log, the comprehensive step tracking app designed to keep you motivated and active.

## Features

### Comprehensive Step Tracking
- View your daily, weekly, monthly, and yearly step totals at a glance
- Track your progress toward your personalized daily step goal
- Monitor distance, calories burned, and active time
- Beautiful, intuitive charts and visualizations

### Goal Tracking & Streaks
- Set and customize your daily step goal (1,000 to 50,000 steps)
- Track your current and longest streaks
- See days with goal achievements and percentage tracking
- Monitor progress since your first goal day

### Activity & Workout Integration
- Seamlessly integrates with Apple Health
- Track all your workouts and activities
- Filter by activity type (running, walking, cycling, and more)
- View detailed workout statistics and routes
- Import GPX files for manual workout tracking

### Detailed Statistics
- All-time totals
- View previous years
- Monthly breakdowns
- Activity rings for calories, exercise, and stand goals
- Customizable data views and sorting options

### Personalization
- Choose from multiple color themes
- Customize background gradients or use your own photos
- Set your preferred units (miles or kilometers)
- Configure week start day and time format
- Create a personalized profile

### Premium Features
- No ads, ever
- Privacy-focused - your data stays on your device
- iCloud sync support (optional)

## Privacy & Health

My Daily Steps Log integrates with Apple Health to access your step and workout data. All data is stored securely on your device with optional iCloud backup. We never sell or share your personal health information.

This app:
- Only reads data from Apple Health
- Stores data locally on your device
- Optional iCloud sync for backups
- No third-party analytics or tracking
- No ads

## Requirements

- iOS 17.0 or later
- iPhone
- HealthKit access required for step tracking
