# My Daily Steps Log

Transform your daily walking into meaningful progress with My Daily Steps Log, the comprehensive step and activity tracking app designed to keep you motivated and active — now on iPhone and Apple Watch.

## Features

### Comprehensive Step Tracking
- View your daily, weekly, monthly, and yearly step totals at a glance
- Track your progress toward your personalized daily step goal
- Monitor distance, calories burned, and active time
- Beautiful, intuitive charts and visualizations

### Goal Tracking & Streaks
- Set and customize your daily step goal
- Track your current and longest streaks
- See days with goal achievements and percentage tracking
- Monitor progress since your first goal day

### Activity Goals
- Set goals for specific activities or all activities combined
- Track by distance, count, duration, or calories
- Flexible date ranges — this year, this month, next month, or custom
- Support for multiple goals per activity with different time frames
- Year-over-year goal history

### Activity & Workout Integration
- Seamlessly integrates with Apple Health to pull in your data automatically
- Track 80+ workout and activity types
- Filter by activity type (running, walking, cycling, hiking, and many more)
- View detailed workout statistics and routes
- Import workout files for manual tracking
- Manage and override workout data directly in the app
- Customize colors for each activity type

### Detailed Statistics
- All-time totals
- View previous years
- Monthly breakdowns
- Activity rings for calories, exercise, and stand goals
- Step totals by year with sorting options
- Customizable data views

### Apple Watch App
- Companion watchOS app with step count, goal progress, and distance
- Circular progress gauge with color-coded goal tracking
- Manual refresh and automatic sync on app open
- Background gradient synced from iPhone app settings

### Apple Watch Complications
- **Corner (Fill)** — step count with a filled progress bar
- **Corner (Dot)** — step count with an open gauge showing 0 to goal
- **Corner (Text)** — step count with "Steps" label
- **Circular** — ring gauge with step count and "steps" label
- **Rectangular** — progress ring with percentage, step count, goal, and distance
- Color-coded progress (red → orange → yellow → green)
- Automatic updates every 30 minutes
- Tapping any complication opens the Watch app with an immediate refresh

### Personalization
- Choose from 25+ color themes including Pro iPhone-inspired colors
- Customize background gradients (dark and light modes) or use your own photos
- Set custom accent colors with full color wheel support
- Per-section color themes for daily, weekly, monthly, and yearly views
- Set your preferred distance units (miles or kilometers)
- Configure week start day and time format (12-hour or 24-hour)
- Create a personalized profile with name and photo

### Guided Onboarding
- Step-by-step setup for profile, preferences, and Apple Health connection
- Live customization preview during onboarding
- Optional data override and workout import prompts

### Tip Jar
- Support development with optional tips
- Preset and custom tip amounts

## Privacy & Health

My Daily Steps Log integrates with Apple Health to access your step and workout data. All data is stored securely on your device. We never sell or share your personal health information.

This app:
- Only reads data from Apple Health
- Stores data locally on your device
- No third-party analytics or tracking
- No ads

## Requirements

- iOS 17.0 or later
- watchOS 10.0 or later (for Apple Watch companion app)
- iPhone (required)
- Apple Watch (optional, for complications and wrist-based tracking)
- Apple Health access required for step and workout tracking
