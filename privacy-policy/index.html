Privacy Policy

My Daily Steps & Activities

Overview

My Daily Steps & Activities ("the App") is developed by an independent developer. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the App accesses and how it is handled.

Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.

The App does not use analytics services, advertising frameworks, or third-party tracking tools. There are no user accounts, and no data is sent over the internet.

Apple Health (HealthKit)

The App integrates with Apple Health to read and display your health and fitness data, including:

  • Step count
  • Walking and running distance
  • Active energy burned
  • Exercise minutes
  • Stand hours
  • Workout history and routes

All HealthKit data remains on your device and is never transmitted externally. Access to HealthKit requires your explicit permission, which you can revoke at any time through the iOS Settings app.

On-Device Storage

All app settings, goals, and preferences are stored locally on your device. The App does not use cloud databases or remote storage.

Camera & Photo Library

The App may request access to your camera or photo library solely to allow you to set a profile picture. Photos are stored locally on your device and are never uploaded or shared.

In-App Purchases

The App offers an optional Tip Jar through Apple's In-App Purchase system. All transactions are processed entirely by Apple. The developer does not have access to your payment information.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
